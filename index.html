About Us

Our research currently focuses on the role of quantum information in quantum gravity and in relativistic field theory. Key topics include quantum black holes, holographic entropy bounds, and the fundamental relation between energy and information.

We are located in the Berkeley Center for Theoretical Physics

For a list of works that the group is thinking about, check our references page.
